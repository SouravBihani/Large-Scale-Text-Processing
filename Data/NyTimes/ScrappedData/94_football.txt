To the Editor:Re “Trump Says He’d Be Wary of Letting His Son Play” (SportsMonday, Feb. 4):President Trump complained to The New York Times’s publisher at their meeting last Thursday that just once he would like to see a positive piece about him. And a couple of days later one actually appeared.The president talked about his fears about his son Barron playing tackle football. He sounded like any other concerned parent. No bluster, no posturing, just serious concern. To the extent that it matters, it was nice to see.Robert RothNew YorkAdvertisement
